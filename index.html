Rust Foundation

A new approach to sustaining and growing a large, participatory, open source ecosystem

Good software is built by happy, well-supported people

The Rust Foundation is an independent foundation focused on the Rust programming language and its ecosystem. We represent a coalition of individuals and organizations that are ready and eager to take responsibility for the privilege of using and participating in open source technology. We support and serve the community of maintainers as they empower a new generation of systems programmers.
